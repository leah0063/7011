06/01/2024

Malacca's famous tourist landmark, Red House, is about to get a brand new sightseeing stopover!

The Melaka Historical City Town Hall has recently created a wooden observation deck outside the Folklore and History Museum at the Red House, allowing visitors to take in the view of the World Heritage Area from a high vantage point.

05/01/2024

Surprises for tourists on the 24th of every month Krabi tourism industry launched the "24 Package".

The Melaka state government and the tourism industry will launch the "Pakej 24" on the 24th of every month, allowing tourists to visit Melaka at a special discounted price, from 10 per cent to 55 per cent, which has received a positive response from many hotels and the tourism industry. Melaka Tourism, Heritage, Arts and Culture Committee chairman Datuk Abdul Razak said domestic and foreign tourists who buy the package will have the opportunity to get the discount.

Melaka New Year's Eve Countdown Concert wraps up with a bang! 15-minute fireworks show to welcome the New Year

Melaka hosted the Neon Dreams New Year's Eve Countdown Concert yesterday. Local artiste Gaston Pong kicked off the event with a number of hit songs to heat up the atmosphere, followed by Suki Low's Malay songs for a perfect start.
