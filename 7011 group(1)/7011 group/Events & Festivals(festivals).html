Indian Pongal Festival

A three-day festival highlighting the colourful, unique and fascinating traditions, heritage, special diets and culture of a handful of Melaka's Chitty communities to mark the end of the harvest season.

the lunar month of Vaisakha

Vesak

Vesak is an annual event in the Buddhist community, symbolising the birth, enlightenment and nirvana of the Buddha. On this day, devotees from all over Malaysia gather at temples of all sizes across the country to offer flowers and incense, as well as chanting scriptures, prayer ceremonies and other customary activities. After nightfall, a series of grand celebrations take place in various cities. The official international name is "United Nations Day of Vesak".

Melaka International Dragon Boat Race

Dragon Boat Racing is a mutually thrilling competition and inspiring race, and every year the Malacca International Dragon Boat Race sees thousands of spectators gather around the Malacca Canal waiting to witness the annual Malacca Dragon Boat Race in person!
