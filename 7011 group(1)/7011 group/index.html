Jan.01.2024 ᐧ

Melaka is the starting point of Malaysia-China friendship and friendship

Melaka is the starting point of the Malaysia-China friendship, and the President of the People's Government of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region of China, Lan Tianli, recorded a video to congratulate him on the 2024 Melaka Tourism Year Countdown Celebration, and arranged for the national actors and acrobats to come to take part in the grand celebrations, which is very meaningful, and it also means that the 50th anniversary of the establishment of the Malaysia-China diplomatic relations was firstly kicked off in Krabi. The 50th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Malaysia and China was the first to be celebrated in Krabi.

Jan.06.2024

Interaction between Kota Kinabalu and Yongchun people deepens traditional friendship between Malaysia and China

Malacca hosted the "Cultural Exchange and Special Products Exhibition of Yongchun County, Fujian Province, China", which not only grandly kicked off a series of activities to celebrate the 50th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries, but at the same time, it was also an opportunity to precisely carry out the practice of the concept of "people-to-people communication, culture first", reflecting the close interaction between the people of Malacca and the people of the two places and the wonderful results of the event. Malacca and Yongchun people's close interaction and good results.

The old houses on the banks of the Krabi River will be given a colourful new look one after another, adding a new charm to the Melaka Tourism Year 2024!

The Buildings Control Department of the City Hall values the old streets and the World Heritage Site and, in partnership with the State Government, is committed to preserving these historic buildings and realising the ambitious vision of the Year of Tourism. "The plan is divided into seven areas for the Old Street renovation project, the first area for renovation is Keeling Street, where 37 flats have already been painted in bright and vibrant colours.
